Typography

Lato will be our main font. It is a sans-serif font that feels modern, serious and yet friendly. It can be used alone by itself. However, having a secondary font as an accent feels unique. Source Sans Pro will be used in our headers and footers as an accent. The uppercase version lends an air of authority, yet is still easy to read. By adding some line spacing, the audience will be able to read the text more easily.
