Logo mark

For the logo mark, we referenced the bottle in which e-liquids are sold in. We added a small, green leaf to showcase the feature of our app which lets you harvest digital plants according to the progress you have made in staying smoke-free and to reference the health aspect of the app.

Logotype

We designed two versions. One is just the logo mark next to the word “Coughie” in source sans pro italic bold. The two work well together because our logo, just like the font type, has no sharp edges and looks friendly to users. It also use the same mint green color that implies health, freshness, and cleanliness. Our other version of a logotype is the word "Coughie" with our leaf logo on the top of the letter "i". This was done to emphasize the plant growing feature of our app again.

Design principles

We hope the shapes and font style communicate to our audience that our app is minimalistic, friendly, and modern, and also has a clean design.

The key design principles we worked towards were contrast, balance, and unity. Our mint green leaf and the dark bottle cap have the most contrast in our design, and the rounded box shape of the bottle creates a sense of balance and unity, as it wraps around all other elements and packages them nicely in a bottle silhouette.
