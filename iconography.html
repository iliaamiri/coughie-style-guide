Iconography

We choose to use icons from Flaticon. We use plant icon and green color to represent the quit vaping is same as planting. The icons withn the plant icons to represent the stage of users quit vaping. We want to show the users that they are not alone in this journey. They are in the same stage with growing plants.

We use this icon to represent the first step of quitting vaping. As a beginner, they need to know they are new to this journey of quitting vaping.

This holding plant icon represents the you are on the right way of quitting vaping.

Seeds represent the users are in the middle of their journey. They are on the way to quit vaping.

This watering plants icon represents the way to quit vaping. Users need to know that they need to water their plants to make them grow.

This icon represents that you have successfully quit vaping.

This icon represents that you have failed to quit vaping.

This icon represents the hamburger menu. Users can click on the hamburger menu to see the menu.
