Iconography

Reasoning

We chose to use icons from Flaticon. We used the plant icon and green color to represent quitting vape and virtual plants. The icons within the plant icons to represent the stage of users quit vaping. We want to show the users that they are not alone in this journey. They are in the same stage with growing plants.

Color

About our icons' colors, we choose #BBC191, #8A5D3D, #F1ECE2 colors to stay consistent with our color palette. Also, we chose some of our clickable icons colorful since they will have color transitions. The other icons are going to be black and white. They are meant to be natural for the users to easily comprehend the meanings behind them.

We used this icon to represent the first step of quitting vaping. As a beginner, they need to know they are new to this journey of quitting vaping.

This holding plant icon represents that the user is on the right way of quitting vape.

Seeds represent that the usr is in the middle of their journey. They are on the way of quitting vape.

This watering plants icon represents the process of quitting. Users will have to water their plants to make them grow.

This icon represents that you have successfully quit vaping.

This icon represents that you have failed to quit vaping.

This icon represents the hamburger menu.

The other icons we will use in our apps

People who use our app should feel comfortable and safe. So we chose the other icons to be rounded, which are simple and easy to understand. We used these icons to represent the features in our apps. We used black and white icons to make sure users can see the icons clearly.
