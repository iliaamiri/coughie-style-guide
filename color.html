About This Palette

Coughie is a website that helps people quit their vaping habit. The colors used in the website are inspired by the colors of nature. The green color is used to represent a leaf, which is the main symbol of the website.

Green is the color of life. it is the color of nature, health, and growth. It is also associated with safety, fertility, and environment. Green represents hope for a better future. It is also relaxing, calming, energetic, and active.

Light blue is peaceful, calming, and relaxing. It is also associated trust, loyalty, wisdom, confidence, intelligence, faith, truth, and heaven. It is also the color of the sky and sea.
