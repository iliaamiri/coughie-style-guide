About This Palette

Coughie is a website that helps people quit their vaping habit. The colors used in the Coughie website are inspired by the colors of nature. The green color is used to represent the leaf, which is the main symbol of the website.

Green is the color of life. it is the color of nature, health, and growth. It is also associated with safety, fertility, and environment. Green is represents hope for a better future. Also, it is relaxing and calming. At the same time, energy and action.

Light blue is peaceful, calming, and relaxing. It is also associated trust, loyalty, wisdom, confidence, intelligence, faith, truth, and heaven. It is also the color of the sky and sea.
